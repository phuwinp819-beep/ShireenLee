Who I am

Hi, I'm Shireen — curious by nature, people-first by design.

I'm a Diploma in Computer Science student at Sunway College, passionate about artificial intelligence, data, and building things that genuinely matter to people.

Beyond code, I've led student programmes, mentored peers, and competed in tech showcases — all driven by a belief that technology is most powerful when it's human. I hold over 17 certifications across AI, data, and professional skills.

When I'm not studying or building, you'll find me learning Thai (slowly 😄), exploring cultural connections, or volunteering at community events.
